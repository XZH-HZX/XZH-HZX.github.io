About Me

My name is ZiHao Xie. I am currently an undergraduate student at Tsinghua University, majoring in MPS (math and physics science) & Energy and Power Engineering. My research interests include natural language processing, autonomous intelligences, and group intelligence. Moreover, I am currently seeking opportunities to pursue a PhD.

I am passionate about exploring the intersection of agents and various fields. Some intuitive phenomena in the biological world could serve as a great starting point for exploring agents.
